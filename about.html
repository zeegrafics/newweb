Muhammad Zahid

My name is Muhammad Zahid. I am a user interaction and experience designer based in Delaware. I have been in the field of graphic, web, multimedia and print design for over a decade. I have found a new passion for user experience, seek to really understand how impactful good UX is for a user, and the important role it plays across all industries. In order to stay current with my design skills, I recently graduated Career Foundry’s UI/UX certified course which helped me stay up to date with the trends in the ever-evolving field. This aided my awareness regarding new trends, technologies, and tools in the professional market.

My future plan is to become a UX designer at a tech company that creates the best solutions for end users and I can play my best role to enhance the product. I want to work in a professional environment where I can improve my skills, learn latest technologies, share my knowledge and where people support each other. I want to work in an environment where my hard work and my skills are acknowledged and I should feel confident about my job status.

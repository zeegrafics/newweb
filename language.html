Project View

  • A very useful language app which is designed to fulfill the requirements of people who want an easy access to foreign languages and Vocabulary.
  • All the languages are lesson based and give very friendly learning environment.
  • Translate It is a very powerful tool which helps the user to translate any word from one language to another and can listen native pronunciation of the translated word.

Need and Goals

Subhan’s Business is situated in the neighborhood where people from different parts of the world live. As the only Convenient store in the neighborhood which full fills almost all the requirements of the neighborhood customers he needs good communication with his customer. To improve his foreign language skills, he is looking for an app which can help him to improve his foreign verbal skills, improve his grammar and help him translating English words to foreign languages.

Proposed Solution

Subhan needs a way to learn different languages to use them with his different foreign customers. He wants to learn daily conversation words and sentences and also wants to translate words from English language to any another foreign language so he can communicate better with the customers and will able to improve his business more. Problem

Problem Statement

We believe that by creating a language learning app with features that provide basic to advance vocabulary, daily useable sentences and ability to translate words from English to any other foreign language will help Subhan to learn his required foreign languages and will create an easy communication with his customers.
